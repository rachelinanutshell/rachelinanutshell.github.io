About

The idea for

He Said, She Said

came about at a time when both Rachel and Sean were seeking a new outlet for their writing. The intention of the site was to create a "dialogue" between the two writers through poetry, and in doing so to help challenge each others' artisitc growth, and ultimately to create a larger examination of the nuances in each author's views and character.

The Authors

Rachel and Sean met at the University of California, Irvine. They are friends and writers based in Northern California.
